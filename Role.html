Roles of ICT

(ICT) plays a vital role in many areas of life, including business, education, and communication.

In Business

Streamlines processes: ICT helps businesses improve efficiency and save money.

Improves communication: ICT helps businesses communicate with employees, customers, and suppliers.

Provides data: ICT helps businesses analyze and store data to make informed decisions.

Helps companies expand: ICT helps businesses expand globally and provide employees with access to company data.

In Education

Facilitates learning: ICT helps students learn at their own pace and access relevant educational materials.

Promotes inclusion: ICT helps students with special needs access essential materials.

Encourages collaboration: ICT helps students, teachers, and experts collaborate and communicate across the world.

Extends learning opportunities: ICT helps students access information and learning opportunities.

In Communication

Builds relationships: ICT helps people build and maintain relationships with others.

Makes communication easier: ICT helps people communicate with each other electronically
